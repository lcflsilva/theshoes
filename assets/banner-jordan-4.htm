      Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” Releases July 3rd

      Air Jordans

      Published on

      Jun 21, 2021
      By Mario Briguglio

      Jordan Brand will be releasing an upcoming Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” that comes in an opposite color blocking as the original “Oreo” Air Jordan 4.

      - Advertisement -

      Dressed in a mix of White, Tech Grey, Black, and Fire Red. This Air Jordan 4 features an all-White tumbled leather upper detailed with speckled Cement Grey on the plastic eyelets and midsole. A solid Grey Jumpman on the heel, Red Jumpman logos on the tongues, insole and outsole atop a White rubber sole completes the design.

      Air Jordan 4 White Oreo Release Date

      Look for the Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” to release on July 3rd at select retailers and StockX. The retail price tag is set at $190 USD.

      Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo”
      Color: White/Tech Grey-Black-Fire Red
      Style Code: CT8527-100
      Release Date: July 3, 2021
      Price: $190

      UPDATE 6/21: Nike confirms that the “White Oreo” aka “Tech White” Air Jordan 4 releases July 3rd via SNKRS. Check out the additional photos via Chris Colgan.

      UPDATE 6/1: A better look at the Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” releasing July 3rd. Check out the additional photos and its packaging below via brandon1an.

      UPDATE 5/10: Originally set to release on June 19th, the “White Oreo” Air Jordan 4 is now listed to launch on July 3rd.

      UPDATE 5/9: In-hand look at the Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” revealing its special packaging releasing June 19th. Check out the photos below via suave95654754.

      UPDATE 4/28: According to brandon1an, the “White Oreo” Air Jordan 4 is being pushed back to June 19th.

      UPDATE 4/2: Jordan Brand has officially unveiled the “White Oreo” aka “Tech Grey” Air Jordan 4 that releases May 29th.

      UPDATE 3/19: Detailed photos of the “White Oreo” Air Jordan 4 have surfaced courtesy of Chris Colgan.

      UPDATE 2/25: Nike reveals official photos of the Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” in adult sizing releasing May 29th.

      UPDATE 2/19: Nike reveals official photos of the Air Jordan 4 “White Oreo” in grade school sizing. The release will be available May 29th in full family sizing. h/t: zsneakerheadz

      UPDATE 10/18: According to zsneakerheadz, this upcoming Air Jordan 4 will NOT be a return of the original “White Cement” and instead it’ll be something new that’s very similar. The photo below is a mock-up of what we could possibly expect from this release. Stay tuned for more updates.
